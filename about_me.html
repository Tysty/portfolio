About Me

Hello! I’m Tyler, a rising sophomore at the University of Utah. I’m majoring in Software Development with a focus on game development. My interest in games started with robotics competitions and programming camps. I enjoyed solving problems and building things that worked. It was fun and gave me a sense of purpose.

Over time, I realized video games combined the creativity of storytelling with the logic of coding. I also saw how games could help people—by building confidence, encouraging teamwork, or just giving someone a moment to relax. That’s what made me want to create games myself—ones that are thoughtful, fun, and meaningful in their own way.
